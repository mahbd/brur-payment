How to pay

Pay via Nagad

  1. Go to your Nagad Mobile Menu by dialing *167#
  2. Press 4 from the menu to select payment
  3. Enter 017XXXXXXXXX
  4. Enter the fee given on notice board
  5. Enter your Roll as reference
  6. Now enter your Nagad Mobile Menu PIN to confirm
  7. Note down your Transaction id and click here

Pay via Bkash

  1. Go to your bKash Mobile Menu by dialing *247#
  2. Choose “Payment”
  3. Enter 017XXXXXXXXX
  4. Enter the fee given on notice board
  5. Enter your Roll as reference
  6. Enter 1 as counter Number
  7. Now enter your bKash Mobile Menu PIN to confirm
  8. Note down your Transaction id and click here

Pay via Rocket

  1. Go to your Rocket Mobile Menu by dialing *322#
  2. Enter 1 to choose “Payment”
  3. Enter XXX as BRUR biller id
  4. Enter your Roll as bill number
  5. Enter the fee given on notice board
  6. Now enter your bKash Mobile Menu PIN to confirm
  7. Note down your Transaction id and click here

Have you paid wrong amount?

  • If you paid more than amount required please contact your department

  • If you paid less than the amount required please rest amount using same method and same account. Also don't forget to provide your roll number as reference. Make a note of your new transaction id and click here
